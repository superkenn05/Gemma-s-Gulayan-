# **App Name**: Gemma's Gulayan

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog Browsing: Users can view the home screen with current location, search bar, promotional banner, product categories with icons, and a popular products section. Browse products in a grid or list layout with images, names, prices, and ratings.
- Product Details Display: Access a dedicated screen for each product, featuring a large image with zoom, product name, rating, price per unit, detailed description, nutritional information, and related product suggestions.
- Search and Filtering: Find specific fruits, vegetables, or other products using a search bar and refine results by applying category filters and sorting options (e.g., by popularity, price, or rating).
- Shopping Cart Management: Add items to the cart, view selected products with quantities and prices, update quantities, remove items, and see a real-time total price calculation.
- Mock Checkout Process: Proceed to a mock checkout flow displaying the total price. This MVP will simulate order confirmation, address selection, and payment method selection, utilizing client-side data for an end-to-end user experience without a backend.
- User Profile and Order History (Mock): A dedicated section for users to view and manage their profile details, delivery addresses, and payment methods. This also includes a UI for displaying a mock order history with status updates.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary accent color: A fresh, deep green (#1C9933) to symbolize nature, freshness, and growth, used for main actions and branding elements. This will contrast cleanly with the light background.
- Background color: A subtle, desaturated green-tinted white (#F0F7F1) providing a clean and expansive canvas, visibly derived from the primary green hue to maintain cohesion while ensuring a bright aesthetic.
- Accent color: A vibrant, bright lime green (#A7EE33) as an analogous complement to the primary green, designed to draw attention to interactive elements and highlights with high contrast.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, neutral, and highly readable characteristics, suitable for conveying product information and ensuring a clear user experience across all text.
- Utilize modern, filled, and rounded icons consistent with Material Design principles (as provided by MUI) to maintain visual consistency and friendliness throughout the application, especially for categories and interactive elements.
- Adopt a mobile-first, responsive design with clear sectioning. Feature a prominent location display and search bar at the top, followed by an engaging promotional banner, category navigation, and scrollable product listings in rounded card layouts. The 'Add to Cart' button is fixed at the bottom for accessibility.
- Incorporate smooth and subtle animations for key interactions, including adding items to the cart and transitioning between different pages or views, to enhance user feedback and overall application fluidity.
